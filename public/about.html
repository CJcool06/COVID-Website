About Tracing App

In conjunction with the government of South Australia and SA Health this web application has be delivered to provide a COVID tracing service to the South Australian Community. This application uses geolocation information to record an accurate, and private record of a user’s check ins. The web application will be updated with ‘Hot Spots’ by SA Health in the event an outbreak occurs within the state. Users will be able to identify their prior check-in locations to determine whether they have been to a known Hot Spot. User’s check-in information will be provided back to SA Health in the event the user returns a positive COVID test result. This will allow SA Health to quickly determine who has been in contact with the user and update the community with up to date information regarding exposure sites and Hot Spots. Wide community adoption and use of this Tracing Application will ensure the safety of South Australians and allow our economy to recover from the pandemic.
