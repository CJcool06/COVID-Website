Use of Personal Data

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, “personal data” refers to all data collected by the COVID Tracing application and its services. The South Australian Chief Health Officer has powers under the Public Health Act to make any directions necessary to contain the spread of COVID-19 within the community. The personal data collected by this application will only be used in accordance with those directions. Examples of personal data being collected include but are not limited to, your name, your email, and your location.

Disclosure or Transfer of Personal Data

This may include your personal information being shared with SA Health officials, or third-party services required to facilitate the service. Personal data will never intentionally be shared outside of Australia.

Data Security

The Group 111 team follow generally accepted industry standards related to online Cyber Security in order to attempt to ensure all data captured by the application is kept secure. However, no system can be completely protected and there is a possibility that your data could be accessed despite the reasonable efforts of the Group 111 team.
